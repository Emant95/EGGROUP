Everest Mineral Water Pvt. Ltd.

Founded in July 2017, Everest Mineral Water is a premier provider of pure artesian mineral water sourced from the pristine Himalayan ranges of Nepal. Harnessing the untouched natural springs, our water is enriched with essential minerals and bottled at the source to ensure the highest standards of purity and freshness. Committed to excellence, we combine advanced technology with sustainable practices to bring the essence of the Himalayas to the world. Everest Mineral Water reflects our dedication to health, quality, and the environment, making it a trusted name in premium hydration.
